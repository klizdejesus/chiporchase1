A Community Effort for Pet Safety

Local shelters are at the heart of pet rescue and safety. Our collaboration with these incredible organizations focuses on providing Chip or Chase trackers for shelter pets, offering a discounted rate for adopters, and organizing educational workshops on pet safety and the importance of pet tracking solutions. Together, we're working to decrease the number of lost pets and support happy reunions between lost pets and their families.
